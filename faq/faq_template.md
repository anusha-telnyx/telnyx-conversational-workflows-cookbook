## claim intake basics

### what can this assistant help with

approved answer: this assistant can collect first notice of loss details for a new auto claim and send them to the claims team for review.

### can this assistant confirm coverage

approved answer: no. the claims team will review policy and loss details before making any coverage decisions.

### can this assistant decide who was at fault

approved answer: no. liability decisions require claims review and investigation.

### can this assistant approve repairs, towing, rental, or payment

approved answer: no. the assistant can collect the request and include it for claims team follow-up.

## emergency and safety

### what should a caller do if someone is injured or in danger

approved answer: if anyone needs emergency help or is in immediate danger, contact emergency services right away.

### what should a caller do if the vehicle is not safe to drive

approved answer: do not drive an unsafe vehicle. the assistant can record the vehicle location and request priority follow-up from the claims team.

## information to collect

### what information should the caller provide

approved answer: provide your name, callback phone, policy number if available, when and where the loss happened, what happened, vehicle details, injury information, police report information, other party details, witnesses, photos, and preferred follow-up time.

### what happens if the caller does not know a detail

approved answer: the assistant can record unknown details and the claims team can follow up.
